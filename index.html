ℹ️ About

SF Cache Quest is a little field-journal-style app built for a family geocaching adventure around San Francisco — Golden Gate Park, the Presidio, Lands End, Glen Park, the Waterfront, and the Richmond District.

Real cache coordinates came from public Geocaching.com listings and GPX exports. The community log, shared caches, and reports are visible to everyone using this link — so be kind, and have fun out there!

Built with ❤️ for Ward & Hyde's next adventure.

🧭 How to Play

1. Start on the Treasure Map. Tap a neighborhood pin to see the caches hidden there.

2. Open a cache card to see its hint, get directions, and check its coordinates.

3. Found it? Tap "Mark Found" to stamp it in your journal and earn points.

4. Share the moment. Post a note to that cache's Community Log — everyone using this app can see it.

5. Add new caches you discover yourself — they're shared with everyone too.

6. Check your Passport anytime to see every cache you've found, and clear a whole neighborhood for a little celebration. 🎉
